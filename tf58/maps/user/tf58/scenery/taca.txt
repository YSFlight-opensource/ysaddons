Taca Island

Taca Island is a fictional island loosely based on Saba Island, part of the Netherlands Antilles in the Caribbean Sea. The airfield has one runway 07-25, bearing 069/249, runway length 1750'. Field elevation 98' ASL. The airfield is day VFR only, and there is no ILS or other navaids on field. There is a VOR on top of the highest peak of the island about 1 mile south of the airfield.
